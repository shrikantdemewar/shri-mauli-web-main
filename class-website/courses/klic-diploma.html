Course Name: MKCL's KLiC Diploma

Learner will be eligible for KLiC Diploma if S/He completes the 3 Courses(each course should be 120 hr) in time span of 1 Year The period of 1 years will be calculated from Date of Issuance of ERA Login of 1st Course till the date of Final Exam Attempt of 3rd Course Learner can complete KLiC Diploma with combination of any 3 KLiC Courses Learner can take admission for all the 3 KLiC courses at a time or as per his/her convenience Learner can do these courses one after another or simultaneously in MKCL's Authorized Learning Center (ALC) Learner has to successfully complete 3 Courses to become eligible for KLiC Diploma within schedule MKCL will provide the KLiC Diploma Certificate to eligible Learner Learner will also get the individual Course Certificates along with KLiC Diploma Certificate
